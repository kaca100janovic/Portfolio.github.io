ORDER FOOD

The application serves as a platform for users and providers, offering a streamlined food ordering and management process. User Profiles: The application caters to two primary user groups: employees and food providers. Employees have individual profiles, granting them the ability to browse the menu and place orders. Provider Functions: Food providers, on the other hand, have more extensive privileges. They can add, modify, or remove food items from the menu, track daily orders placed by employees, and view comments and ratings left by users. This project requires rigorous testing to ensure a seamless user experience for both employees and providers. My role as a manual tester involves meticulously examining the application's functionality, assessing user interfaces, and verifying that all features, such as order placement, menu management, and user feedback, operate smoothly and effectively.
